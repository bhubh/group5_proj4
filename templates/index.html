Future of US Renewable Energy

With the recent climate bill passing this weekend in the house and senate, we wanted to analyze the current production of clean energy and the distance away from our realistic reliance on clean energy as the U.S. main energy source. We realize that there is a need for cheap clean energy sources, but we also realize that it could be costly to transition away traditional sources of energy like fossil fuels and natural gas. We attempted to design a machine learning model that uses linear regression to predict the amount of clean energy production and consumption will be over the next two decades.
